About

As with every programmer there comes a day that they have an issue learning something new. For some visual guides explain the material better than written explanations

This site started as a way to prove that I knew what I was doing as well as noticing that as I taught Web Development I seemed to explain things the same way over and over and with horrible self drawn images. I found that some of the images actually helped my students and began to think they could help others as well - even those I didn't teach
